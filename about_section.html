About The Founder

With an inspiring journey that spans over two decades, Sandeep Tripathi is a unique blend of scientific temperament and spiritual insight. He is not only a highly respected astrologer and vastu expert, but also a dedicated NEET Chemistry educator who has worked with India's most renowned academic institutions for the last 20+ years. His mentorship has helped thousands of medical aspirants successfully enter top government and private medical colleges across the country. Many of his students are now practicing MBBS doctors, serving society with distinction.

Sandeep Tripathi's astrological journey began in 2003 when he was awarded the "S. Ratnesh" degree in Vedic Astrology by the Padmesh Institute of Vedic Sciences, Kanpur, in a felicitation ceremony graced by then Commissioner Mohammad Haleem Khan. Since then, he has trained under India's topmost astrology mentors, mastering rare and time tested techniques from diverse systems including Parashari, KP, Bhrigu Nandi Nadi, and Tajik Varshphal.

Over the past 22 years, he has diligently applied and verified thousands of classical and modern astrological formulas on real-life horoscopes, ensuring each prediction stands the test of time and experience. His deep understanding of divisional charts, dasha analysis, transits, AstroVastu, and karma-based remedies makes him one of the most reliable and result-oriented astrologers in the field today.

At AstroTripathi, he offers personalized and insightful consultations on career, marriage, health, education, finance, business, foreign settlement, and legal issues, blending practical logic with spiritual wisdom.
