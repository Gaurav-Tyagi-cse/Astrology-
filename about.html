AstroTripathi is a proud MSME-registered Indian enterprise, committed to reviving and applying the profound wisdom of India’s ancient sciences—Astrology and Vastu—for the benefit of modern society. Our mission is rooted in integrating traditional spiritual frameworks with practical contemporary solutions, especially for businesses, corporate sectors, and individuals seeking direction, growth, and alignment with natural energies.

At AstroTripathi, we specialize in multiple authentic and time-tested systems of astrology and vastu, including:

  • Vedic Astrology (Parashari System)
  • Bhrigu Nandi Nadi (BNN)
  • Krishnamurti Paddhati (KP System)
  • Tajik Varshphal (Annual Prediction System)
  • Classical Vastu and Mahavastu
  • AstroVastu Integration Techniques
