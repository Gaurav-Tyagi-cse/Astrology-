  • privacy policy

Last Updated: December 2024

AstroTripathi is committed to protecting your privacy and maintaining the confidentiality of your personal information, birth chart data, and consultation details.

Information We Collect

Personal Information: When you book a consultation or enroll in our courses, we collect your name, email address, mobile number, gender, and current address to provide personalized services.

Birth Chart Data: For astrological consultations, we collect your birth date, exact birth time, and birth place. This information is essential for accurate horoscope preparation and predictions using Parashari, KP System, Bhrigu Nandi Nadi, and Tajik Varshphal techniques.

Consultation Records: We maintain records of your consultation sessions, questions asked, predictions provided, and remedies suggested to ensure continuity of service and follow-up support.

Payment Information: For transactions, we collect billing information through secure payment gateways. We do not store your complete credit card or banking details on our servers.

How We Use Your Information

Astrological Services: Your birth data is used exclusively for preparing accurate horoscopes, conducting divisional chart analysis, calculating dasha periods, and providing personalized predictions and remedies.

Business Consultations: For corporate clients, we analyze company foundation charts and key personnel birth charts to provide strategic business guidance while maintaining complete confidentiality.

Educational Services: Student information is used for course delivery, progress tracking, and certification purposes in our Vedic Astrology, KP System, and other specialized training programs.

Communication: We use your contact information to schedule appointments, send consultation reports, share educational content, and provide customer support.

Data Protection and Confidentiality

Professional Ethics: As per traditional Vedic astrology principles and modern professional standards, all client information is treated with utmost confidentiality. Your birth chart data and consultation details are never shared with third parties.

Secure Storage: All personal and astrological data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption and security measures. Access is restricted to authorized personnel only.

MSME Compliance: As an MSME-registered enterprise, AstroTripathi adheres to Indian data protection regulations and maintains professional standards in handling client information.

Staff Training: All team members are trained in data privacy protocols and are bound by confidentiality agreements to protect client information.

Sharing of Information

Strict No-Sharing Policy: We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information, birth chart data, or consultation details with any third parties, including other astrologers, marketing companies, or data brokers.

Legal Requirements: Information may only be disclosed if required by law, court order, or government authorities, and only to the extent legally mandated.

Service Providers: Trusted third-party service providers (payment processors, email services) may have limited access to necessary information solely for service delivery, under strict confidentiality agreements.

Your Rights and Choices

Access and Updates: You can request access to your stored information and ask for corrections or updates at any time by contacting us.

Data Deletion: You may request deletion of your personal data, though we may retain consultation records for professional reference and follow-up services as per astrological practice standards.

Communication Preferences: You can opt-out of promotional communications while still receiving important service-related messages about your consultations or courses.

Consultation Records: You have the right to receive copies of your consultation reports and birth chart analysis upon request.

Cookies and Website Technology

Our website uses cookies to enhance user experience, remember preferences, and analyze website traffic. You can manage cookie settings through your browser, though some features may be limited if cookies are disabled.

We use Google Analytics and similar tools to understand website usage patterns while ensuring no personally identifiable information is shared with these services.

International Data Transfers

As AstroTripathi primarily serves clients in India and follows traditional Vedic astrology practices, your data is processed and stored within India. For international clients, data transfer is conducted with appropriate safeguards to ensure privacy protection.

Children's Privacy

While we provide astrological services for minors with parental consent, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without explicit parental permission. Birth chart consultations for minors require parent or guardian approval.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Significant changes will be communicated via email or website notifications. Continued use of our services after updates indicates acceptance of the revised policy.
